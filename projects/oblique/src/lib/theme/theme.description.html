This is an internal feature of Oblique and should only be used when live theme or font change is required. The vast majority of projects won't need this service and should just stick to the configuration options.

The desired theme, either oblique-material.css or oblique-bootstrap.css, should simply be loaded in angular.json's assets array, after oblique-core.css. Alternatively, it could also be defined through the setTheme method of the ObThemeService. But do not use both ways together as it will lead to 2 separate themes being loaded simultaneously which will yield unpredictable results.

The desired font, either frutiger.css or roboto.css, should simply be loaded in angular.json's assets array. Alternatively, it could also be defined through the setFont method of the ObThemeService. But do not use both ways together as it will lead to the font being loaded twice which will yield unpredictable results.

Because of licence restrictions, the Frutiger font files cannot be delivered with Oblique. Each project has to obtain the font files itself from webforum@bk.admin.ch or the federal chancellery intranet and put them into the src/assets/fonts folder. Each project is also responsible for the font protection according to its licence. The proposed solution consist of only delivering the font if the Referer Http header is whitelisted.
